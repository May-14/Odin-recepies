Oatmeal Muffin Recipe

Description

This oatmeal muffins make for a light, healthy, and delicious breakfast that you can take anywhere. Fast and easy to make, they make a great start to your day; full of whole grains, they'll provide you with the energy you need in what ever adventure your day takes you. Mouth-watering delicious and soft as a cloud, you won't believe that something as healthy can taste so good.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup sour cream or Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1/4 cup applesauce
  • 1/2 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 cup quick cooking oats or old-fashioned oatmeal pulsed in a blender
  • 1 cup mix-ins (optional: chocolate chips, dried fruit, nuts, etc.)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Grease one standard 12-cup muffin tin. Set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl, mix together flour, baking powder, baking soda, sea salt, and cinnamon, set aside.
  4. In a large bowl, whisk together sour cream, melted butter, applesauce, milk, brown sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  5. Add the dry ingredient mixture and stir to combine.
  6. Stir in the oatmeal.
  7. If desired, stir in 1 cup add-ins (chocolate chips, nuts, dried fruit, etc.)
  8. Pour Â¼ cup batter into each well of the prepared muffin tin.
  9. Bake 17-19 minutes, or until the tops spring back when touched and a cake tester inserted in the center comes out clean.
  10. Let cool in the muffin pans for 20 minutes before removing with a spoon or sharp knife.
  11. Transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
