Cookie recipe

Description

Everyone knows, and loves, chocolate chip cookies, but today's recipes often leave much to be desired. They either try to reinvent the wheel or go with too simple of a cookie. The balance between this two is where the perfect cookie lies, and where we'll be cooking today.We'll be making the cookie we all imagine when thinking of one: crunchy but chewy, soft but solid, and most importantly sweet and delicious.

Besides being utterly delicious, the recipe is also extremely easy; no cooking experience is required! Ready to get into this recipe? My mouth is watering just writing this, let's start!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup salted butter softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar packed
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips (14 oz)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Line three baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. In a medium bowl mix flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Set aside.
  3. Cream together butter and sugars until combined.
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla until light (about 1 minute).
  5. Mix in the dry ingredients until combined.
  6. Add chocolate chips and mix well.
  7. Roll 2-3 Tablespoons (depending on how large you like your cookies) of dough at a time into balls and place them evenly spaced on your prepared cookie sheets.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for approximately 8-10 minutes. Take them out when they are just barely starting to turn brown.
  9. Let them sit on the baking pan for 2 minutes before removing to cooling rack.
